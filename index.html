Instantly append your transactions directly to a Google Sheet from your mobile phone!

Status: Not Synced

Quick Setup (2 Minutes):

  1. Create a new Google Sheet.
  2. In your Google Sheet, go to Extensions > Apps Script.
  3. Paste the following code, then click Deploy > New deployment.
  4. Select type "Web App", set Access to "Anyone", then authorize & deploy.
  5. Copy your deployment's Web App URL and paste it in the box above!
function doPost(e) {
  var sheet = SpreadsheetApp.getActiveSpreadsheet().getActiveSheet();
  var params = JSON.parse(e.postData.contents);
  
  // Appends row: Date, Period, Type, Amount, Category
  sheet.appendRow([
    params.date,
    params.period,
    params.type,
    params.amount,
    params.category
  ]);
  
  return ContentService.createTextOutput(JSON.stringify({ "result": "success" }))
    .setMimeType(ContentService.MimeType.JSON);
}

How to Use

This is a super fast offline-ready utility designed for lightning quick manual input logging on your mobile device!

1

Set your transaction details: Type in your numerical amount first, select the category from the ledger dropdown, and toggle between Spend (-) or Income (+).

2

Hold the central wheel: Press and hold down with your finger anywhere inside the central circle for a continuous 2 seconds to log it.

3

Add new sheets: Use the plus symbol in the bottom sheets bar to quickly make a new monthly ledger. All data stores automatically in your browser storage or syncs with your configured Google Sheets spreadsheet.
